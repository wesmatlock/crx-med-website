    <h2>What CRX Is</h2>
    <p>CRX is an iOS app for tracking medications — schedules, reminders, refills, symptoms — and for safely sharing that picture with the people who help you. It's built for real people managing real regimens: older adults, anyone living with a chronic condition, and the caregivers supporting them.</p>

    <h2>Our Mission</h2>
    <p>CRX was built with a simple belief: <strong>medication management should be calm, clear, and private.</strong></p>
    <p>Most medication apps are either too complex, built for clinicians instead of patients, or quietly monetize your health data. CRX aims to be the one that makes sense for real people — reliability over novelty, clarity over clutter.</p>

    <h2>Design Principles</h2>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Calm over urgency:</strong> No alarming colors or anxious UI. Medication management is already stressful enough.</li>
      <li><strong>Accessibility first:</strong> Not an afterthought — a core requirement from the first line of code.</li>
      <li><strong>Privacy by design:</strong> No ads, no tracking, no selling data. Sharing happens only with your consent, and you can delete your account and data in the app at any time.</li>
      <li><strong>Clarity always:</strong> Every label, every action, every screen should be unambiguous.</li>
      <li><strong>Honest about limits:</strong> CRX is a tool, not a doctor. It says so, plainly, wherever it matters.</li>
    </ul>
